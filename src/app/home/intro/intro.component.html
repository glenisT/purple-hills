Purple Hills is a Synth Rock duo formed in 2017, in Tirana, Albania by Glenis Tivari and Trebla Muso. 
            Their sound is characterized by a blend of different genres of Rock and Electronic music, creating a unique sound with elements of Synthwave, Rock, 
            Metal, Downtempo, and Experimental. They released their debut album, "Escapism" on the 18th of December 2021, 
            a completely independent and original 
            project written and produced by the duo themselves.
